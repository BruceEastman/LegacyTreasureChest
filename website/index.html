Legacy Treasure Chest

A private, AI-assisted household inventory and estate planning companion.

Legacy Treasure Chest helps individuals and families catalog possessions, organize important household records, and generate structured reports that can assist with estate planning, downsizing, or liquidation.

The system was designed to provide advisory guidance while keeping personal information private and under the user's control.

Key Principles

  • Your inventory is stored locally on your device.
  • The app provides advisory guidance, not transactions.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • The system does not maintain a central database of estates.
